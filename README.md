## Why High Protein Foods Matter for Muscle Building

Protein is essential for muscle repair and growth. When you train, your muscle fibers experience small tears. High-protein foods supply the nutrients needed to rebuild these fibers stronger than before.

### Key Benefits:
- Supports muscle growth and repair  
- Improves recovery after workouts  
- Helps increase strength and performance  
- Reduces muscle breakdown during training

## How Much Protein Do You Need for Muscle Building?

For optimal muscle growth:

- **1.6 to 2.2 grams of protein per kg of body weight per day**

Example:
- 70 kg person → 112g to 154g protein daily

## Best Time to Eat High Protein Foods

- **Breakfast:** Kickstarts muscle protein synthesis  
- **Post-Workout:** Enhances recovery and growth  
- **Evenly throughout the day:** Maintains steady amino acid levels
